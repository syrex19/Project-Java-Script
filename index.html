Ferrari 599GTO Fiorano Scuderia


Beutifully handcrafted grand tourer styled by Pininfarina under Ferrari's lead designer Ken Okuyama. The 1/18 scale representation of this masterpiece is completed by BBR. No introduction is needed for this italian model company who produces some of the most breathtaking replicas in the hobby world of diecast collectibles. Some of the details on this model are prevalent in the interior where real leather is used. As for the the exterior the perforated grill, cross drilled brakes and Ferrari branded calipers painted in red are delighful to look at.

Mercedes 300SL convertible


This 1957 classis is sure to please the eyes of any collector. Even if you don't fancy the Mercedes brand, this classic is sure to capture those hearts. Measuring at just over 9.5 inches long and painted in this metallic black paint. The details continues in bonnet as well with photo etched wiring. The inline 6 cylinder engine is masterfully displayed with a meticously painted gray paint to represent the aluminium block. This model will look good in any environment, even outdoors as its pictured.

Lancia Delta


This 1979 Delta Lancia is a rare breed of cars. Produced in 3 generations up until 1992 it competed in rally racing and on road racing and many won many of them. Some inviduals perceived this car as Italy's Subaru. This white paint shines in the sun and the wheels as the catch the glare of the sun. There's detail in every part of the vehicle starting from top to bottom. A proper engine bay with matching decals and color scheme is a feast to gaze upon! The windshield wipers are fully operational, along with the doors, bonnet and hatch. Dare I say who wouldn't want this car model in their collection?
